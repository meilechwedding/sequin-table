Sequin's primary call-to-action — uppercase, wide-tracked, pill-shaped, with a warm gold bevel and a lift-on-hover. Use for actions like "Explore Rentals" or "Shop Now".

```jsx
<Button variant="primary" size="lg">Explore Rentals</Button>
<Button variant="outline" iconRight={<i data-lucide="arrow-right" />}>Shop Home</Button>
<Button variant="ghost" size="sm">View Cart</Button>
```

Variants: `primary` (champagne foil — default, hero CTAs), `solid` (espresso, on light), `feature` (bordeaux, special moments), `outline` (gold hairline, secondary), `ghost` (tertiary/nav). Sizes: `sm | md | lg`. Pass `as="a"` with `href` for links. Keep labels 1–3 words, Title Case or short imperatives.
