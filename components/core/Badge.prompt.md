Small uppercase label for status and metadata — product flags, rental tags, counts.

```jsx
<Badge>New</Badge>
<Badge variant="feature">Rental</Badge>
<Badge variant="neutral">Sold out</Badge>
<Badge variant="sage">In season</Badge>
```

Variants: `gold` (champagne foil — default), `feature` (bordeaux), `neutral`, `outline`, `sage`. Keep to 1–2 words.
